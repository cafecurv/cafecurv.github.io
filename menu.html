Frequently Asked Questions
How do I place an order?▼
Browse the menu and tap + on any item to add it to your cart. For sized drinks tap R+ or L+. Fill in your details, tap Copy Order Message, then tap Open Messenger — paste and send! That's it. 🎉
Where do you deliver?▼
We have our own rider covering Brgy. Santiago and Brgy. San Francisco in General Trias, Cavite. For areas outside our vicinity, we use Lalamove — just select it as your delivery type and the fee is shouldered by the customer.
What payment methods do you accept?▼
We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), GCash / Maya, and Bank Transfer. For electronic payments, full payment is required before your order is processed. Payment details will be sent via Messenger.
What are your operating hours?▼
We operate from morning until midnight. Hours may vary — follow our Facebook page @curvsideph for daily availability updates.
Where is your matcha from?▼
We use First Flush Ceremonial Grade Matcha sourced directly from a farmer in Kirishima, Kagoshima Prefecture, Japan. It's a blend of Yabukita, Tsuyu Hikari, Okumidori, and Saemidori cultivars — low bitterness, nutty, creamy, and slightly floral. 🍃
Do you have allergen information?▼
Our products may contain allergens including wheat, eggs, dairy, nuts, and seafood. Please message us via Messenger before ordering if you have any allergies or dietary restrictions and we'll be happy to help.
How long does delivery take?▼
Delivery time depends on your location and order queue. Our own rider covers nearby barangays quickly. For Lalamove orders, estimated time will be shown in the app. We'll keep you updated via Messenger. 🚗
Can I request less ice or no sugar?▼
Absolutely! Use the Special Requests field in the order form. Note that less ice or no ice for matcha drinks will result in a non-full cup to maintain taste balance.
