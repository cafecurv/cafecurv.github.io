Our Story
The Curv

Where the name came from.

Curv started in a small garage in General Trias, where Japanese simplicity meets French creativity — creating a style that’s both refined and thoughtfully made.

The name comes from the tagline — "where uncertainty meets possibility." Like a curve on a road, life never goes in a straight line. Success doesn't either. But somewhere in that bend, that unexpected turn, you find the best part of the journey.

At The Curv, every cup is intentional. Every plate is styled. Every order is handled like it's the only one. Because this isn't just a cafe — it's proof that the curved path, the one less traveled, is where the magic happens.
