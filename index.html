Our Story
The Curv

Where the name came from.

Curv wasn't born in a boardroom. It started in a garage in General Trias with a dream, a matcha whisk, and zero safety nets. Just a kid who left a stable job to chase something he couldn't yet explain.

The name comes from the tagline — "where uncertainty meets possibility." Like a curve on a road, life never goes in a straight line. Success doesn't either. But somewhere along those unexpected turns, you find the things that matter most.

At The Curv, every cup is intentional. Every plate is styled. Every order is handled like it's the only one. Because this isn't just a cafe — it's proof that the curved path is worth taking.
